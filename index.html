Positioning

Healthcare is an operations problem, a people problem, and a data problem at the same time.

Most leaders are strong in one of those dimensions. My work has lived at the intersection of all three. I started where care actually happens — registering patients in an emergency department and serving as an Epic super user — then moved through workforce analytics, enterprise compliance and governance, operational performance improvement, and enterprise EHR and revenue cycle transformation.

That path means I read a performance problem the way an operator does, model it the way an analyst does, and move it the way a change leader has to. I understand healthcare from the front line through executive strategy — and I translate between those worlds.

Leadership Philosophy

Leadership is multiplying capability — in systems and in people.

I lead from a servant-leadership foundation: my job is to remove friction, build clarity, and develop the people and processes around me so the work outlasts me.
