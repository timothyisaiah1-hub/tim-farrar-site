An operating leader who has worked at every layer of a health system — from emergency registration to enterprise transformation — combining operations, analytics, technology, revenue cycle, and leadership development to improve access, cost, and patient experience.

Executive Profile

I understand healthcare from the front line through executive strategy. I diagnose performance problems like an operator, model them like an analyst, and move them like a change leader. My work spans patient access, revenue cycle, governance and risk, Lean Six Sigma performance improvement, enterprise EHR transformation, and the development of future healthcare leaders.
